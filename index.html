About Us

Crystal Carat DMCC, headquartered in Dubai’s DMCC Free Zone, specializes in trading gold, silver, and other commodities. Backed by strategic investors, we provide a gateway to responsible sourcing, transparent pricing, and global reach. Our operations are legally compliant and adhere to the highest standards of security and ethics.

Our Commodities

Gold

Crystal Carat offers LBMA-certified gold bullion to institutional and retail clients. Our global sourcing and secure logistics ensure high integrity and purity, with flexible investment and hedging options.

Silver

From investment bars to high-purity industrial silver, we provide sovereign and private mint products. We also support clients with secure vaulting, transport, and bulk silver handling solutions.

Palladium

Palladium from Crystal Carat is tailored for both industrial and investor applications. Our platform ensures direct access to refined palladium with customizable logistics support.

Copper

As we expand our base metals portfolio, Crystal Carat facilitates physical copper trades with reliable sourcing, LME compliance, and delivery assurance to fabricators and manufacturers.

Lithium

Positioned to support the energy transition, Crystal Carat now offers trading solutions in lithium—critical for battery technology and electric mobility. Our team sources high-grade lithium from vetted suppliers globally.
